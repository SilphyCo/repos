Mum's Special Pizza

A delicious & classic plate waiting to be dished to your loved ones

No artificial ingredients or pre-made sauces here—just fresh dough, a vibrant medley of vegetables, quality cheese, and herbs come together to create a wholesome, mouthwatering pizza that’s perfect for sharing with family and friends.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour (for the dough)
  • Active dry yeast
  • Warm water
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Fresh garlic (minced)
  • Fresh tomatoes (diced)
  • Red bell pepper (thinly sliced)
  • Mushrooms (sliced)
  • Red onion (thinly sliced)
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Parmesan cheese (for finishing)
  • Oregano (dried or fresh)
  • Crushed red pepper flakes (optional, for some heat)

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough: In a large bowl, dissolve the yeast in warm water and let it sit for 5 minutes until foamy. Add flour, olive oil, and salt. Mix and knead for about 8–10 minutes until smooth and elastic. Cover and let it rise in a warm spot for 1–1.5 hours, until doubled in size.
  2. Preheat the oven: Set your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it inside to heat.
  3. Make the fresh tomato base: In a bowl, combine diced tomatoes, minced garlic, a pinch of salt, olive oil, and oregano. Let it sit for 10 minutes to let the flavors blend.
  4. Shape the dough: Punch down the risen dough and roll it out on a floured surface into your desired pizza shape and thickness.
  5. Assemble the pizza: Spread the fresh tomato mixture evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust. Layer the sliced red bell peppers, mushrooms, and red onion on top. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese generously over the vegetables. Add a few fresh basil leaves and a pinch of crushed red pepper flakes if you like a little heat.
  6. Bake: Transfer the pizza carefully to the preheated oven or pizza stone. Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  7. Finish and serve: Remove from oven, sprinkle freshly grated Parmesan cheese on top and add extra fresh basil leaves for garnish. Slice and serve hot. Enjoy your homemade classic pizza with loved ones!
